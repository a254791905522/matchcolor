Match 3 Color Puzzle is built with your privacy as a top priority. The developer does not collect any personal data, usage data, or analytics from this application. Your gameplay stays entirely on your device.

This Privacy Policy describes how Match 3 Color Puzzle ("the App", "we", "us", or "our") handles your information. We believe in full transparency about our data practices and want you to feel confident while enjoying our color-matching puzzle game.

We have designed Match 3 Color Puzzle to function without collecting any data from you. Specifically, we do not collect:
